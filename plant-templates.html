02 · Image + Text

Sunlight

Light Is Life

Most houseplants thrive in bright, indirect light. South- or east-facing windows are ideal. Too little light causes leggy growth; too much scorches leaves.

03 · Feature Card
💧
Watering

When to Water

Stick your finger 2–3 cm into the soil. If it's dry, water deeply until it drains from the bottom. If it's damp, wait. Overwatering is the #1 killer of houseplants.

04 · Image Top
🪴
01

Choose the Right Pot

Always use a pot with drainage holes. Terracotta breathes, plastic retains moisture — pick based on your plant's needs and your watering habits.

05 · Text + Image
🌱

Soil Matters

A quality potting mix with perlite or orchid bark ensures good aeration. Most houseplants prefer slightly acidic, well-draining soil.

🌍
06 · Step Guide
🌿

Repotting in 3 Steps

1

Gently remove the plant

Squeeze the pot sides and slide the root ball out. Loosen any circling roots with your fingers.

2

Add fresh soil

Place a layer of fresh potting mix at the bottom of the new pot. Position the plant at the same depth as before.

3

Water and settle

Fill around the roots with soil, tap gently, and water thoroughly. Let it drain before placing back.
